Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small community meetup for technology enthusiasts in Lagos, Nigeria. Over the years, it has grown into one of the leading global technology conferences, hosting thousands of attendees and showcasing innovative breakthroughs in AI, robotics, software development, and more.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, empower young minds, and foster collaboration among professionals in the global tech ecosystem. We believe technology is a powerful tool for solving real-world problems and creating a better, connected future.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the brightest minds in technology, business, and innovation. Here are a few of our remarkable past speakers:

Dr. Ada Lovelace

A pioneering AI researcher and data scientist, Dr. Lovelace’s groundbreaking work in ethical AI frameworks has shaped how organizations approach responsible innovation.

Elon Musk

As the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, Elon Musk has revolutionized space travel and renewable energy. His insights at TechCon 2019 inspired thousands to pursue futuristic technology ventures.

Dr. Fei-Fei Li

Dr. Li, co-director of the Stanford Human-Centered AI Institute, shared her vision on integrating empathy into artificial intelligence for a more humane digital future.
